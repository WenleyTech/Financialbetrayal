Categories

Clear categories help survivors recognize what happened, reduce self-blame, and find relevant research and support.

Core Categories

  • Romance Fraud & Trauma Bonds
    Trust-based manipulation in romantic contexts, often paired with coercion and secrecy.
  • Coercive Control & Financial Abuse
    Restriction, surveillance, threats, dependency-building, and control over money/identity.
  • Family Financial Exploitation
    Parent/relative misuse of access, pressure, guilt, cultural obligation, or “eldest daughter” dynamics.
  • Affinity & Investment Betrayal
    Exploitation via shared identity/community ties; promises of returns or legitimacy.
  • Legal Retaliation & Court-Related Trauma
    Retaliatory lawsuits, intimidation, and prolonged conflict that compounds trauma.
  • General Financial Betrayal Trauma
    Betrayal that doesn’t fit neatly elsewhere but follows similar trust-and-harm patterns.

How to Use This Page

1) Identify your closest category.

2) Read the relevant research and language frameworks.

3) Use the resources page for grounding and next steps.
