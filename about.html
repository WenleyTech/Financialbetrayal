About FBRC

Independent research and education—built for clarity, survivor validation, and pattern documentation.

Mission

FBRC documents patterns of trust-based financial exploitation and its psychological impact. Our goal is to provide clear language, frameworks, and accessible resources so survivors can name what happened—and make informed next steps.

Methodology

Research is derived from publicly available records (including court filings and civil litigation), credible reporting, and survivor accounts.

We organize patterns into categories and publish plain-language summaries that reduce confusion and self-blame.

Boundaries

FBRC does not provide therapy, diagnosis, legal services, or clinical treatment. We provide educational resources only.

The BackBeam Connection

Survivors seeking trauma-informed mental health support may visit BackBeam Network, a specialized therapist directory. FBRC publishes educational research; BackBeam provides a pathway to professional care.
