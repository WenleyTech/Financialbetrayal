About FBRC

Independent research and education—built for clarity and survivor validation.

FBRC is an independent research initiative dedicated to documenting and defining patterns of trust-based financial exploitation and its psychological impact. Our goal is to provide clear language, frameworks, and accessible resources so survivors can name what happened—and make informed next steps.

DISCLAIMER: The Financial Betrayal Research Center provides research and educational resources only. We do not provide therapy, diagnosis, legal services, or clinical treatment.

If you are in crisis, please contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or 911.

For therapy services, visit BackBeam Network.
