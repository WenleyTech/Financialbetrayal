What is Financial Betrayal Trauma?

Financial Betrayal Trauma describes what happens when financial harm is inflicted through a relationship of trust (romantic, family, professional, or community-based). The money matters ‚Äî but the defining feature is the breach of trust, the manipulation, and the destabilization that often follows.

Our Approach

FBRC research is derived from publicly available records (including court filings and civil litigation), credible reporting, and survivor accounts. We provide educational resources only.

We do not provide therapy, legal services, diagnosis, or clinical treatment.

Survivors seeking mental health support may visit BackBeam Network, a specialized therapist directory for Financial Betrayal Trauma.
