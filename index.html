About FBRC

Independent research and education‚Äîbuilt for clarity and survivor validation.

FBRC is an independent research initiative dedicated to documenting and defining patterns of trust-based financial exploitation and its psychological impact. Our goal is to provide clear language, frameworks, and accessible resources so survivors can name what happened‚Äîand make informed next steps.

All research is derived from publicly available records, court filings, and survivor testimony. We do not provide therapy, legal services, or clinical diagnosis. Our role is pattern documentation and education.

For therapy services, survivors may visit BackBeam Network, a specialized therapist directory built exclusively for Financial Betrayal Trauma.

DISCLAIMER:

The Financial Betrayal Research Center provides research and educational resources only. We do not provide therapy, diagnosis, legal services, or clinical treatment.

If you are in crisis, please contact 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or 911.

For therapy services, visit BackBeam Network.
