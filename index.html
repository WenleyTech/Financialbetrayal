Understanding Financial Betrayal Trauma

The Financial Betrayal Research Center (FBRC) is an independent research initiative dedicated to documenting and defining patterns of trust-based financial exploitation and its psychological impact.

Read the Research Find a Therapist →

Our Mission

  • Document behavioral patterns of financial exploitation
  • Provide research-backed frameworks
  • Create accessible educational resources
  • Establish clear trauma categories

Our Approach

FBRC research is derived from publicly available records including court filings, civil litigation, and survivor accounts. We do not provide therapy, legal services, or clinical diagnosis.

Survivors seeking mental health support may visit BackBeam Network, a specialized therapist directory for Financial Betrayal Trauma.
